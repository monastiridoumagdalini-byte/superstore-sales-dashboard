# Superstore Sales Dashboard (Tableau)

## Overview
Interactive Tableau dashboard analyzing Superstore sales data to explore trends in sales, profit, categories, regions, and product performance.

## Key Insights

### 1. Sales by Category
Furniture and Technology drive the majority of total sales, while Office Supplies contribute significantly less, indicating an imbalance in revenue contribution across product categories.

### 2. Regional Performance
Sales performance varies across regions, with certain regions consistently outperforming others, suggesting geographic disparities in market efficiency and demand.

### 3. Product Contribution
A small subset of products contributes disproportionately to total sales, indicating a high concentration of revenue among top-performing items and potential dependency on key products.
